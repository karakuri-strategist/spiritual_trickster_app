title: Tittytwister

<pre>
People just can’t go straight,
They’re twisted, they’re twisters.
So be careful where you go around twisting,
Don’t give yourself a tittytwister!
Unless you’re into that,
Then do you mind if I help?

No? Ok!
Yes? Yes!
</pre>
