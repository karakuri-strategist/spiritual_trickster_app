title: Isaac's Son
pubDate: 2022-12-19
---

<pre>
Isaac - 
The names meaning
“The one who laughs.”

But in this world
Where the son is sacrificed 
To the image of the father
Again
And again,
I ask,
Truly,
What joy is left?
Who is smiling?
Who is laughing now?
</pre>
