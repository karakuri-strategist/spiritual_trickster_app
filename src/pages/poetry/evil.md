title: 'Evil'
pubDate: 2022-12-19

<pre>
Cruelty,
Oh cruelty
Mischief,
Oh mischief
Oh,
The tears I’ve cried.

Evil,
Oh Evil, 
Demons,
Oh demons,
Oh,
How I’ve wished you to die.

But I will wait,
Oh wait and see
What all this pain has bought -

I will see
Oh wait and see
Oh,
What Evil has wrought.
</pre>
